Purpose

The Purpose of the site is to promote our activities as a white wter rafting company. The site will inform people of what we offer and also serve to showcase the experiences of groups we have taken through tours. It will also provide people with contact details so they may make bookings.

Audience

The Target audience for the site is basically everyone with a desire for thrills, whether its adrenaline junkies of just firstime rafters. We have packages for all ages and a wide range of ability. When they visit the website they will see the available touring packages and select the one they desire. They will also be able make bookings or find conact information. They will mostly be using mobile devices to access the site.
